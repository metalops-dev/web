# What is Metalops

Hello there! Welcome to my first blog post.

Metalops is a technical writing space focused on software, systems, infrastructure and devops/mlops/llmops from low level to production.

I am particurally interested in:
- Building systems that are resilent before they are clever
- Choosing interesting solutions when they scale better and not try to make it boring
- Homelabbing and self-hosting
- Play around with different technologies and build fun and interesting projects
- Build and deploy baremetal solutions

Metalops is not a news feed and it’s not a hype channel. Posts here will be slower, opinionated, and grounded in practical experience. Some articles will be technical deep dives. Others will focus on architecture decisions, system design tradeoffs, and operational lessons learned.

Over time, I plan to document:
- Architecture  experiments
- Design patterns that have aged well
- Decisions I would not repeat
- Tools that earn their place

Welcome to Metalops.
